Problem Solving

1. Tell your non-tech friend a story about a time you:

what was the problem?
what problem-solving techniques did you use?
how did you feel throughout the process?
what did you learn?

I was working on the javascript kata challenge and got stuck with the super flip flop and gradebook challenges. I got stuck because I overcomplicated the logic and used the wrong method. I tried the rubber ducky method by talking through the problem step-by-step and used AI as a tutor to talk me through the problem. I also took a short break to clear my mind before trying again. At first, I felt frustrated and overwhelmed. I learned that taking a break and talking through the problem helps me see things more clearly and avoid overthinking.
